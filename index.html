Our Story

We are getting married.

During our time together, we have experienced many travels and challenges, from food adventures in the alleys of Tainan to romantic trips in Japan. Each experience has deepened our love. We have learned to support and understand each other in every little aspect of life, growing together. Our love story began in the bittersweet days of our school years, stood the test of time, and has now blossomed beautifully.
Now, we sincerely invite you to join us at our wedding, to not only celebrate our love but also to honor the incredible journey that transformed our friendship into a profound love.

在交往的日子裡，我們一起經歷了許多旅行和挑戰，從台南的小巷美食探險到日本的浪漫自由行，每一次的經歷都讓我們的愛更加深厚。我們在生活的點點滴滴中學會了互相支持和理解，共同成長。我們的愛情故事，從青澀的校園時光開始，歷經時間的考驗，最終開花結果。
現在，我們誠摯地邀請你們，來參與我們的婚禮，不僅慶祝我們的愛情，也慶祝那份從友情轉化為深厚愛情的奇妙旅程。
